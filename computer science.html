About Us

With the vision of holistic development of students and grooming them into vibrant professionals well-grounded in knowledge, skill and confidence in the field of computer science, the Department of Computer Science offers B.Sc. (Hons) Computer Science, BA (Programme) Computer Applications and provides Computer Science as one of the disciplines in B.Sc. Mathematical Sciences. Core papers like Operating Systems, DBMS, Computer Networks, Data Structures along with latest subjects like Python, JAVA, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Internet Technologies are taught as a part of curriculum to B.Sc. (Hons) Computer Science and B.Sc. Mathematical Sciences. The courses match the requirements of professional software engineers and open a wide range of academic avenues like MCA, M.Sc. and MBA. The department also offers BA(Programme) Computer Application course which is mainly for non-science students. The course promotes computer literacy and programming skills so that the students gain basic skills for entry level jobs in information technology. The students are taught using various ICT techniques. The faculty is well qualified, experienced and responsive to the academic disparities in the background of the learners. Many of our students have been successfully placed in various companies, including Deloitte, TCS, FIS Global and Wipro
