About Us

Welcome to the English department of PGDAV Evening College. Presently, the department has eight permanent faculty members while two teachers are serving on ad hoc basis. The department caters for numerous courses of different classes. Besides compulsory papers, it offers a wide array of elective courses. It touches the life of every student of the college in some semester or the other.
The department has an active ‘English Literary Society’ that organizes activities relating to language and literature for students. It also oversees the English

Vision

The department is a predominantly young department. But the youthful enthusiasm goes with adequate qualifications and skills. The faculty members not only participate in mandatory academic programmes but also keep themselves updated through research and publication in their individual areas of interest. Members of the department are also associated with the ‘Innovative Projects Scheme’ of the Delhi University as mentors.
